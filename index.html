Photography

Sunset in Morocco

Gazing upon the sea of dunes cascading around me, I wonder how far can I walk upon this sand. Will I overcome the harsh sun and reach my camp? Will the rays sink me into the golden abyss? No matter the result: the only answer will come with one step forward.

Diamond Lights

No lights. I stand, enveloped by darkness. My vision alive only by the sea of stars above, gleaming faintly upon them. The silent, cold air pricks my skin, hardening it with every sharp gust. My lens stares into the glimmering abyss above, recording every light that sparkles before it collapses in exhaustion. The sky twirls the earth gently, and they dance together for eternity.

Thin Flames

Sparks dance around my body, crashing and scorching the wooden earth. Water surrounds me to save me from an infernal accident, but with proper care, I thrive.
